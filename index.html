ADDITIVE MANUFACTURING
×
An additive manufacturing workshop provides a hands-on environment for participants to learn about this exciting technology and its potential applications in industry, research, and beyond may also learn about the different types of materials used in 3D printing, such as plastics, metals, and ceramics, and how to select the appropriate material for their needs.
3D PRINTING
×
Participants in a 3D printing workshop may learn about the various types of 3D printing technologies, such as fused deposition modeling (FDM), stereolithography (SLA), and selective laser sintering (SLS), and how to use them to produce high-quality prints. They may also learn about the different materials used in 3D printing, such as plastics, metals, and ceramics, and how to choose the appropriate material for their needs.
CFD
×
In a CFD(Computational fluid dynamics) workshop, participants learn how to use CFD simulation software to solve fluid flow problems, such as predicting the performance of a fluid system or the behavior of a fluid structure interaction. The workshop may also cover various topics related to fluid dynamics, such as turbulence modeling, boundary conditions, and post-processing techniques for data visualization.
